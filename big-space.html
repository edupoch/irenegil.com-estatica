BIG SPACE

The starting point is the L shaped wall in the exhibition space
which is extruded by the use of white elastic ropes.
6 elastic lines are connecting 3 walls.
6 elastic lines that link the outside and inside exploring limitations of the given space.
The intervention fuse different times and different spaces, creating a dynamic playground.
A big space, a big mess.

Year: 2015

Technique/ Medium: Elastic ropes. Installation.

Team: Peter Vink
